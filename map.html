Vishvarupa – Vision of the Cosmos

Vishvarupa is the name of the divine experience in which the entire creation is manifested in the complete form of one supreme consciousness. According to Vedic philosophy, the universe is not merely a collection of physical matter, but a gradual manifestation of consciousness. This Vedic map explains that spiritual structure through images. The transcendent Purnabrahma is the supreme, absolute, and transcendental reality — beyond time, space, and causation. From it, the eternal reality of Akshara Brahma manifests; Akshara Brahma is the foundation of the divine abode and the conscious realms. Kevala Brahma is the form of the subtle cause of creation, while Akshara Brahma is the expansive system of the fourfold consciousness. Kshara Purusha is that changing world which we experience — bound in the cycle of birth, growth, and destruction. This map is not any geographical map, but rather the philosophical form of existence.
